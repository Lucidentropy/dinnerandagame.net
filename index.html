Welcome to Dinner and a Game

Dinner and a Game started as most of these things do—as a home gaming group. We had a blast playing together and wanted to share that joy with others, so we created a podcast—and it grew from there. Now we create content to support our geek and gaming community.

At Dinner and a Game we will always save you a seat at the table.

Events and Shows

The games we play.

Emerald Sanctums

Tuesdays 7:30 PM CST on Twitch or our other streams

How do you get a ADHD werewolf, a felon, a noble, poly fae with 3 spouses, a 300 year old weregator and a former Wizard Lizard to save the world? Find out every Tuesday evening with our World of Darkness Multi-Splat game. Join our found family as they try and keep each other safe in the Emerald Sanctums.

Kingdoms of Mist

Wednesdays 8 PM CST on Twitch or our other streams

A new Campaign Setting being developed by Dinner and a Game! Come join our players as we explore new world's and try and learn the secrets of The Mistmother to save the Kingdoms of Mist.

The Walking Dead Jazz Funeral

Every other Friday 8 PM CST on Twitch or our other streams

In the Big Easy it's not always a party. When the dead walk and the swamp wants to take it's land back it will be time for a Jazz Funeral. Join us as we play a game that pushes the limits of your comfort. Be warned this is a very adult themed show with strong horror themes.

Open Source Life, Big Dev Dreams

Every other Friday 8 PM CST on Twitch or our other streams

Nightcity is no joke. You gonna make your mark or you gonna be a mark on the street? They gonna eat you alive or you gonna get ate? This is where Cyberpunk 2077 started with the TTRPG! Join us as we play Cyber Punk Red the latest edition of the TTRPG as our edgerunners do their best to cut their own legend out of the shady back streets of Nightcity.

Meet our DaaGer Crew!

These people are the heart and soul of our Dinner and a Game creative team—from players to storytellers, they're the reason DaaG's stories feel so personal, relatable, and amazing. We introduce you to the DaaG Family:

Sphinx Akashaa

A self-proclaimed Champion of Bards, Sphinx is most definitely a Neurodivergent, Queer, CRIT Award Nominated, BIPOC TTRPG Player, Storyteller, Musician, Writer, and Content Creator walking the Storied Path. He's successfully completed a 6-year-long Eberron AP podcast and is soon launching a new AP TTRPG Anthology Podcast called The Everbright Archive!

FountainPen Femme

Milagros (She/They) is a queer, intersex, Latine/x, neurodivergent and disabled author, actor, and TTRPG player. Tank Lesbian main, meticulous note-taker—even in one-shots!—and based in New York, they juggle twenty projects when not scrap-booking or reading tarot.

Markov_and_co

Markov.and.Co—Sam for short—is a TTRPG, cosplay, and gaming enthusiast with a past in military service, theatre, and improv. An AuADHD-fueled creator famous for gut-wrenching emotional dives, endless innuendo, and lightning-fast, outside-the-box plans.

SelkieCove

Delving into all things creepy-and-cute, Selkie Cove (He/They) is a queer, neurodivergent, disabled TTRPG player and creator. Eldritch darkness meets pastel kawaii in 3–5 streams per week—and they're always scouting new projects.

Wrenound

Wren (They/Them) is a queer, Latino, non-binary larp/TTRPG/card-game designer, maestro of disaster, and chaos conduit. Expect deep personal engagement, emotional content, and joyful wreckage in every game.

Starshard.Stories

Disabled demigirl content creator, cosplayer, storyteller, and lore-diver with 24 years of tabletop under her belt. When she's not fussing at tiny cat Bean, she giggles—or cries—her way through streams alongside her spouse.

Ladee Vamp

Kassy (She/Her) is a Minnesota-based cosplayer and 5e devotee. Chronically procrastinating, neuro-spicy, and incapable of self-care—but always ready to dive head-first into storytelling.

Myss Lyss 2

Cosplayer, DM, and chaos engine. From “level-up lesbian” to “cutest bean” werewolf, Myss_Lyss keeps players—and GMs—on their toes.

The Hipster GM

Coco (They/Them) is a lore-loving content creator famed for ttrpg, autism, and nerd culture videos. When not smithing metal scrap into art or wandering forest trails, you'll find them playing themselves in Jazz Funeral.

KelticWyvernKing

TTRPG player/GM and content creator (He/Him). On-screen in one DaaG show and running an ever-growing roster of games on his own channel.

Vixie

Your friendly neighborhood GM and player. Latina, grant recipient, and Games on Demand GM for multiple 2024 cons—producing actual-play content at Pocket Dragon Pub and DaaG alike.

GeekGirl Lyssa

Demifemme, queer content creator. Streams games and community chats on Twitch, shares gaming & cosplay snippets on TikTok, and champions social equality.

DiceGoblin BlackCat

Dice maker with goblin energy—appearing in campaigns and one-shots alike. When not rolling math rocks she's disrupting the patriarchy and plotting an escape from Texas to cooler climes.

Hythrall

TTRPG creator and cosplayer (She/Her) who conjures detailed OCs like magic. Catch her every other Friday in Open Source Life, Big Dev Dreams playing the stoic Chryst.

Diamond Lynn

All-round TTRPG experience—AP performer, setting writer, schedule wrangler, and Keeper of Spines. Diamond keeps the DaaG machine running.

Shepard

Twenty-three, non-binary, and perpetually crying over fictional characters. Shepard writes short stories, edits video, and wants every table to roll dice and tell fun stories.

Mark J

40-year-old boy, actual-play producer, writer, and founding member of Apochromatic Unlimited. Host of the nOne-shOt with Mark J podcast, uplifting BIPOC in TTRPG spaces.

MrDaaGShow

Founder of the feast. Ray (He/Him) is older than D&D itself and still eager to lecture after exactly four minutes. He launched DaaG to share the joy of this hobby and give back to the community.

MrsDaaGShow

Life-long gamer, introvert, and one of DaaG's two founders. She named the channel and has spent countless hours growing its social media presence (and Ray is madly in love with her).

BishiBunny

DaaG's only VTuber bun—disabled, queer, and always low on blood. Loves complex mechanics, intricate stories, and more campaigns than time allows.
